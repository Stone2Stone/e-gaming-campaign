During their 2023 annual voyage, Innovation Island was discovered by SCC with the help of its open-source partner, Red Hat. Here SCC and Red Hat found a home for three of their products; Red Hat Enterprise Linux, Red Hat OpenShift, and Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform. After months of construction, this unknown island is now open to the public to explore and discover. Come and discover the endless possibilities that Innovation Island has to offer.

  1. Explore all three buildings to discover each product.
    Auditorium: Red Hat Enterprise Linux
    Arcade: Red Hat OpenShift
    Control Room: Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform
  2. Each building contains power-ups to expand your knowledge.
    Collect all of the power-ups in each room to enter a prize draw to win a Lego Lego Globe Set. Each room completed is worth 1 entry into the prize draw on the 30th April 2023.
  3. Innovation Island’s public library is always
    open to curious people.

Now you know all you need to make the most of Innovation Island.

HOW TO PLAY

To move around the Island simply use the arrow keys. When you need to interact with a person or item press the spacebar.

It’s as easy as that.
